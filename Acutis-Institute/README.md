# The Saint Carlo Acutis Institute

Planning workspace for **Acutis Institute** — an independent, AI-native, high-velocity Catholic micro-school under the patronage of Saint Carlo Acutis. The concept marries the Superbuilders / Alpha 2-Hour Learning platform with a curriculum and culture faithful to the Magisterium and the classical Christian tradition.

## Concept at a Glance

- **Patron:** Saint Carlo Acutis (canonized 2025) — the millennial coder-saint who built digital catechetical platforms for the Church.
- **Model:** AI-driven academic mastery in the morning; afternoons devoted to virtue formation, real-world projects, and mastery of life categories.
- **Governance:** Independent 501(c)(3) lay apostolate. Canon-803-compliant naming preserves operational velocity without diocesan ownership or financial entanglement.
- **Brand Architecture:**
  - *The Saint Carlo Acutis Institute* — legal & canonical name
  - **Acutis Institute** — public-facing brand
  - *Acutis* — everyday shorthand

## Strategic Thesis

The **Catholic Alpha**: the first AI-native, high-velocity Catholic micro-school framework. Aligned with Superbuilders/Alpha core tenets (2-Hour Learning, extreme motivation, high-leverage AI tools), with **faith and the pursuit of virtue as the motivational engine**. Where Alpha uses tech/entrepreneurship and Texas Sports Academy uses athletic advancement, Acutis Institute uses the pursuit of spiritual greatness.

See [References/acutis_school_strategy.md](References/acutis_school_strategy.md) §4 for the full pitch framework targeting Patrick Skinner and Superbuilders leadership.
