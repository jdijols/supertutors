- [Rerum Novarum (1891)](https://www.vatican.va/content/leo-xiii/en/encyclicals/documents/hf_l-xiii_enc_15051891_rerum-novarum.html) — *Pope Leo XIII* — landmark encyclical on capital and labor; the namesake foundation for Pope Leo XIV's *Magnifica humanitas*
- [Magnifica humanitas (forthcoming, May 25, 2026)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Magnifica_humanitas) — *Pope Leo XIV (Wikipedia)* — his first encyclical, on preserving the human person in the age of artificial intelligence
